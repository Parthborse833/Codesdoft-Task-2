The Local Train is an Indian rock band formed in Chandigarh in 2008, and based in New Delhi since 2015.[1] The band's current lineup consists of lead guitarist - Paras Thakur, bassist - Ramit Mehra, and drummer and percussionist - Sahil Sarin. In April 2022, the band announced that their vocalist / frontman Raman Negi has left the band.
